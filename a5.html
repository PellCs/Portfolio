Sun and Moon

This program offers a captivating day and night cycle that users can switch between by clicking on the sun and moon. Inspired by nature's beauty, I carefully selected colors and shapes to create a peaceful atmosphere. The interactive features allow users to click on the celestial bodies, transitioning seamlessly between day and night. Implementing cloud movement and appearance presented a challenge, but I successfully resolved it by creating an array of cloud objects that update their position and size with each frame.
