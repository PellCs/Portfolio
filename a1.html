Self-Portrait

Click on the Image

I curated a collection of captivating images, including night skies, game consoles, a sleek vehicle, and the iconic Goku character from Dragon Ball. Each image represents my unique interests and passions. Integrating these seven images into a cohesive composition posed the challenge of maintaining visual harmony while allowing the car and character to stand out. The result is a visually captivating reflection of my personal inclinations.

Personal reflection of myself, carefully crafted to make a bold statement through vibrant colors. These colors were deliberately chosen to accentuate my presence and create a visually striking effect. They hold deep personal significance, as I have a genuine fondness and strong attraction to them. Naturally, the primary inspiration for this artwork was none other than myself, serving as the subject and driving force behind its creation.
