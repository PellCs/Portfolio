Happy Emotion

I embarked on an animation project to bring a word to life through color transitions, aiming to convey the essence of Happiness. The color selection was carefully curated, encompassing a wide spectrum of hues. Alongside the vibrant colors, I incorporated a bouncing circle to add dynamism, as it presented a compelling challenge to animate with an authentic bounce effect. Throughout the process, I encountered the challenge of familiarizing myself with shortcuts and discerning which properties governed specific elements, but this hurdle was overcome with persistence and practice.
