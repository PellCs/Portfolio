New York City Subways

The backdrop for this captivating sound piece is none other than the bustling metro system of New York City. It serves as a reflection of the city's vibrant chaos and the myriad of emotions experienced by its inhabitants on a daily basis. A familiar scene to all, the presence of talented musicians performing in the subway resonates through the nearby Manhattan stations. To recreate this ambiance, I skillfully curated five distinct audio samples, artfully utilizing the eco and reverb effects while delicately adjusting the volume levels of each track. The true challenge lay in harmonizing the elements in a way that no single sound dominated the composition, allowing each track to be discernible and appreciated individually amidst the symphony of sounds.
